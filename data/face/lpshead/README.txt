Original data were downloaded from https://casual-effects.com/data/
Direct link: https://casual-effects.com/g3d/data10/research/model/lpshead/lpshead.zip

head_triangulated.obj* were made from the original data by the following processes:
1) Convert mesh from quad to triangle by MeshLab.
2) Annotate 8 landmark vertex ids manually.
3) Resize diffuse texture from 4K to 1K
